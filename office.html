About Us
NEXT architecture provides the full range of architectural and interior design services. These services include: strategic and master planning, programming, design, image and branding consultation, project management and engineering consultation. We pride ourselves in the ability to provide clients with a broad range of experience, a strong work ethic and the commitment to service from start to finish on each project.

We have no ego: every project is unique; it allows us to ask “what’s next?” always looking for new ideas, always looking forward. We do not chase every project that comes our way: our size allows us to be selective of project type and number. It is important to us to maintain principal involvement, actual production of work product, to offer our clients the type of client service we would expect. We do not take opportunities that test us lightly: we revel in the challenges that open our mind and expand our talents.

Daniel J. Delisio
AIA, Principal / Owner
Dan has over 30 years of experience in all areas of architectural practice. Dan has spent the seven years prior to forming NEXT architecture as a principal and shareholder of The Design Alliance Architects in Pittsburgh, PA. Throughout his career, Dan has guided clients and project teams on projects ranging in size from $100,000 to $50,000,000. Dan has successfully completed projects for commercial office, health care, retail and light industrial office clients in both new and renovation construction projects. Dan’s resume includes numerous large relocation projects that were strategically planned and programmed by teams he directed or was personally involved with.
